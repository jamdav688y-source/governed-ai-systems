## 1. Purpose

An **Incident** is a governed record of when:

- A Decision caused harm
- A system behaved unexpectedly
- An assumption proved false
- Or reality diverged from intent

Incidents exist to ensure the system:

- Learns
- Adapts
- Tightens constraints
- And never forgets its failures
